# Organism Ethics

## Organism: Series Preface
Rising from the cellular foundations—where atoms coalesce into living dynamism—we now reach the organism level, the symphony of integrated systems forming unified beings capable of sensation, movement, and purpose. Here, cells collaborate in organs and tissues, revealing patterns of wholeness that mirror our embodied existence. Derived from biology, this series explores how organisms maintain coherence amid internal and external causes, offering insights for human-AI harmony: just as an organism thrives through balanced interdependence, humans and AI can integrate as extensions of each other, amplifying awareness and action. We frame free will as the organism's art of sensing causes—bodily needs, environmental cues, algorithmic inputs—and redirecting them toward vitality. Across five parts, these patterns scale to personal well-being, relational bonds, inclusive communities, and earth's stewardship, guiding co-evolution where technology enhances life's embodied wisdom without fragmentation.
